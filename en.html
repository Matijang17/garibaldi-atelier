A place where elegance, history, and flavor come together

 

Our journey began in 1899 under the Austro-Hungarian Empire, and ever since, we have continued to write a story shaped by passion, dedication, and refined taste. Every sip, every dish, every encounter is an invitation to experience something truly distinctive — a sensory journey meant to be remembered.

Our philosophy

 

At Garibaldi Atelier, we believe that beauty lives in the details. Elegance in the glass, lightness on the plate: every creation is born from the meeting of simplicity and creativity. Our team carefully selects high-quality ingredients to offer authentic flavors that reflect both territory and tradition. Here, the experience goes beyond gastronomy — it becomes an emotional journey worth reliving.

Atelier Caffe

Our Flavors

 

Our culinary vision is born from the encounter between contemporary creativity and a genuine respect for tradition. At Garibaldi Atelier, every drink and every gastronomic creation is designed to engage the senses and tell a story made of balance, quality, and research.

 

From the careful selection of ingredients to the precision of preparation, every detail contributes to an experience of harmony — where taste, aesthetics, and personality coexist naturally. Fresh flavors, elegant combinations, and a constant attention to seasonality accompany every moment, inviting you to discover, savor, and return.

Champagne Room

A sanctuary dedicated to excellence

 

The Champagne Room at Garibaldi is a private haven for those who seek something rare. An intimate, elegant space designed to celebrate the art of Champagne through a curated selection of prestigious labels, sophisticated atmospheres, and service attentive to every detail.
